# Workbench

The Workbench is where new material begins life.

Nothing on the Workbench is considered published. It is a staging area for observations, fragments, drafts, references, images, artifacts, and ideas before they are ready to graduate into the public website.

## Rules

- Capture first.
- Organize lightly.
- Polish later.
- Nothing on the Workbench is considered published.
- Material graduates from the Workbench into the website when it is ready.

## Workflow

```text
Observation
      |
      v
Workbench
      |
      v
Review
      |
      v
Field Note / Workshop Journal / Cabinet / Exhibit / Tribute
      |
      v
Published
```

## Workbench Areas

- `field-notes/` - early durable observations, reference notes, and reusable knowledge.
- `workshop-journal/` - early process notes, experiments, repairs, and build records.
- `cabinet/` - early object, artifact, source, tool, book, and reference notes.
- `exhibits/` - early exhibit drafts before they meet `docs/exhibit-standard.md`.
- `tributes/` - early memorial or remembrance material requiring privacy and permission review.
- `photography/` - early image selections, captions, contact sheets, and photo notes.
- `ideas/` - loose questions, sparks, and unsorted future possibilities.
