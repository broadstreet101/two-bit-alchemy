# Brand Manifest

This document is the long-term creative and design constitution for Two-Bit Alchemy. It preserves the approved direction and should be used to evaluate future creative, content, and implementation decisions.

## 1. Core Identity

Two-Bit Alchemy is a personal identity and website project centered on curiosity, observation, craft, and patient transformation.

It is broader than Fisher Aquatics. Fisher Aquatics is one project within the larger Two-Bit Alchemy world, alongside other projects, field notes, workshop posts, collections, and reflections.

The site should feel personal, exploratory, grounded, and durable rather than corporate, sales-focused, or trend-driven.

## 2. Guiding Question

The website should answer:

> What has Dada been curious about lately?

This question takes priority over:

> What services does this business sell?

## 3. Design Philosophy

The design should support observation, recordkeeping, understanding, and making.

Approved themes:

- Observe.
- Record.
- Understand.
- Create.

Two-Bit Alchemy represents transformation through observation, craftsmanship, patience, and curiosity.

## 4. Visual Style

The approved visual direction is Victorian natural-history engraving.

Influences:

- Scientific plates
- Field journals
- Cabinet-of-curiosities references
- Antique laboratory details
- Botanical and celestial study
- Warm ivory paper tones

The mood should be curious, grounded, handcrafted, and quietly mystical.

Visual execution should favor excellent typography, strong photography, carefully used illustration, calm layouts, and minimal animation.

## 5. Voice And Tone

The voice should be personal, reflective, curious, and specific.

It should feel like a thoughtful field journal or workshop notebook, not a resume, sales page, or corporate brochure.

Preferred qualities:

- Clear
- Warm
- Observant
- Patient
- Handmade
- Knowledgeable without showing off

## 6. Site Experience Principles

The site experience should be calm, readable, and easy to explore.

Approved principles:

- Use a warm ivory background or similarly calm reading surface.
- Prioritize typography and legibility.
- Keep animation minimal and purposeful.
- Use photography and illustration as meaningful content, not filler.
- Make project areas easy to browse without flattening everything into a business pitch.
- Separate workshop progress posts from evergreen field notes.
- Let the site reward curiosity and wandering.

## 7. Approved Symbolic Elements

Approved emblem and symbolic elements include:

- Triple Moon
- Draco constellation
- Polaris
- Microscope
- Antique camera
- Axolotl
- Botanical elements
- Appalachian stream
- Candle
- Notebook
- Mortar and pestle
- Subtle pentacle
- Exposed roots

Approved future refinements:

- Make Draco feel like an authentic celestial engraving.
- Reduce remaining geometric diagrams.
- Remove or soften constellation labels.

## 8. Things To Avoid

Avoid:

- Corporate visual language
- Sales-first positioning
- Fantasy magic
- Steampunk
- Horror
- Excessive animation
- Trend-driven visual effects
- Resume-like About content
- Treating Fisher Aquatics as the whole identity
- Replacing the approved direction without an explicit decision

## 9. Objective Vs Subjective Decision Rules

Objective improvements are changes that measurably or clearly improve:

- Accessibility
- Readability
- Performance
- Security
- Maintainability
- Content clarity
- Navigation
- Preservation of approved project decisions
- WordPress stability and recoverability

Subjective preferences are changes based mainly on taste, mood, or aesthetic judgment, such as:

- Exact color shade
- Typeface personality
- Illustration density
- Decorative detail level
- Layout rhythm
- Degree of visual ornament

Decision rule:

When recommending a change, identify whether it is objective or subjective. Objective improvements may be recommended directly with rationale. Subjective changes should be framed as options and should not overwrite approved direction without approval.

## 10. ChatGPT Vs Codex Responsibilities

ChatGPT serves as Creative Director.

ChatGPT responsibilities:

- Develop and refine creative direction.
- Guide voice, tone, symbolism, and content framing.
- Help evaluate whether ideas fit the approved identity.
- Preserve authenticity over flash.

Codex serves as Software Architect and Implementation Engineer.

Codex responsibilities:

- Preserve existing successful decisions.
- Make incremental implementation improvements.
- Audit WordPress before redesign work touches the live site.
- Recommend themes, plugins, templates, and technical structure when appropriate.
- Assist with CSS and implementation details after approval.
- Optimize performance, accessibility, maintainability, and security.
- Maintain the implementation log after each coding or repository session.
- Distinguish objective improvements from subjective preferences.

Shared responsibility:

- Plan before coding.
- Work iteratively.
- Avoid unnecessary changes.
- Keep documentation concise enough for future AI handoffs.
- Do not begin website implementation without approval.
